WHY ME?

Graphic Designer
As a designer who specializes in branding and social media, I help clients create a distinctive and appealing identity for their businesses by designing logos, fonts, colors, graphics and other visual elements that match their vision, values and goals. I have experience in producing high-quality and original content for different platforms and audiences, using various tools and techniques. I work with clients to understand their needs and expectations, and I deliver solutions that make them stand out from the competition and attract their ideal customers.

Video Editor / Animator
As a creative video editor, animator and motion designer, I have the skills and experience to produce engaging and captivating visual content for various platforms and purposes. Whether it is a short film, a documentary, a commercial, a social media post or a presentation, I can apply my artistic vision and technical expertise to edit, animate and design videos that suit the needs and expectations of my clients. I am proficient in using various software tools such as After Effects, Photoshop and Illustrator, as well as other industry-standard applications. I am always eager to learn new techniques and trends in the field of video editing, animation and motion design.

UI/UX
I am a UI/UX designer with a passion for creating user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing interfaces. I have recently started my career in this field and I am eager to learn from experienced professionals and improve my skills. I have a background in graphic design and animation, and I enjoy applying my creativity and problem-solving abilities to various projects. Some of the tools I use are Figma,HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
